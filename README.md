# Retail Sales Dashboard — Power BI

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard gives a clear visual picture of retail sales performance across products, customers and time periods. Built to help a business owner answer key questions at a glance without digging through raw data.

## Business Questions Answered
- What is total revenue and how does it trend month to month?
- Which products and categories are performing best?
- Who are the top customers by spend?
- Which cities generate the most orders?
- Where are the gaps or underperforming areas?

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 — Sales Overview
- Total Revenue KPI card
- Total Orders KPI card
- Average Order Value KPI card
- Monthly Revenue trend line chart
- Revenue by Category bar chart

### Page 2 — Product Performance
- Top 5 Products by Revenue
- Units Sold by Product
- Underperforming Products table

### Page 3 — Customer Insights
- Top 5 Customers by Spend
- Revenue by City
- Customer Order Frequency table

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel (data source)
- DAX (calculated measures)

## Key Insights
- Hampers category drives over 60% of total revenue
- December is the highest revenue month
- Johannesburg and Pretoria are the top two markets
- 3 products flagged as underperforming for review
